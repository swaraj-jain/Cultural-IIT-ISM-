FotoFreaks

Fotofreaks - The Photography Club Of ISM is a group of photography-enthusiasts here at ISM and has two major objectives. Firstly, to act as a platform for bringing together the budding photographers of ISM and providing them with opportunities for honing their skills. Secondly, it aims to be a collection of all sorts of ISM memories, from photographs of serene roads to those of gigantic halls filled with loud, enthusiastic students; from photographs of the quiet, peaceful Heritage building during dawn to those of students sweating under the sun in rallies. We believe that photographs are a beautiful way of capturing moments and preserving memories, they take you back in time and make you rekindle the lost elation that you repeatedly feel during numerous experiences throughout your ISM life.
