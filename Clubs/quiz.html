Quiz Club

Did you know that the IST Line passes through Kaleen Bhaiya's city?
Or that a real plane was crashed while shooting Tenet, because it was cheaper than CGI?
Or that Sachin wasn't the first person to score an ODI double century?
Do you like watching movies or binging shows? Playing games or listening to music? Only to be told they aren’t hobbies?
Well, Quiz Club IIT ISM thinks they are.
We at Quiz Club are all about taking things we love and finding out what makes them cool.
The perfect club for anyone who loves movies, books, shows, sports, business or science. We like facts and trivia of all things we find interesting. Here nobody will ask you who the prime minister of Uzbekistan is or what is the capital of Cambodia. We don’t like boring things and we hope you don’t like them either. Quiz Club aims to provide you the opportunity to channel your love of trivia!
